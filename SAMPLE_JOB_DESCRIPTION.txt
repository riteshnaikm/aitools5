Job Title: Senior Full Stack Developer

Company: PeopleLogic Technologies

Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

About Us:
PeopleLogic Technologies is a leading HR technology company specializing in AI-powered recruitment and talent management solutions. We're on a mission to transform how companies find, evaluate, and retain top talent.

Role Overview:
We're seeking an experienced Senior Full Stack Developer to join our growing engineering team. You'll be responsible for designing and developing scalable web applications that power our AI-driven HR platform, working with cutting-edge technologies and making a real impact on how companies hire.

Key Responsibilities:
• Design, develop, and maintain full-stack web applications using modern frameworks
• Build RESTful APIs and integrate with third-party services
• Implement responsive, user-friendly interfaces with React/Vue.js
• Work with AI/ML teams to integrate intelligent features into the platform
• Optimize application performance and scalability
• Participate in code reviews and mentor junior developers
• Collaborate with product managers and designers to deliver features
• Ensure code quality, testing, and documentation standards

Required Qualifications:
• 5-7 years of experience in full-stack development
• Strong proficiency in Python (Flask/Django) and JavaScript (React/Vue.js)
• Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
• Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
• Understanding of RESTful API design and microservices architecture
• Experience with version control (Git) and CI/CD pipelines
• Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
• Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Preferred Qualifications:
• Experience with AI/ML integration or NLP libraries
• Knowledge of Docker and Kubernetes
• Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies
• Previous experience in HR tech or SaaS products
• Contributions to open-source projects

Technical Stack:
• Backend: Python, Flask, FastAPI
• Frontend: React, Vue.js, JavaScript/TypeScript
• Database: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis
• Cloud: AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda)
• AI/ML: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face
• DevOps: Docker, Jenkins, GitHub Actions

What We Offer:
• Competitive salary (15-25 LPA based on experience)
• Performance bonuses and stock options
• Flexible work hours and hybrid model
• Health insurance for you and your family
• Learning and development budget
• Modern office with latest tech
• Collaborative and innovative culture
• Career growth opportunities

Interview Process:
1. Initial screening call (30 minutes)
2. Technical assessment (coding challenge)
3. Technical interview with engineering team (1 hour)
4. System design discussion (1 hour)
5. Cultural fit interview with leadership (30 minutes)
6. Final offer discussion

How to Apply:
Send your resume and portfolio to careers@peoplelogic.com

Application Link: https://peoplelogic.com/careers/senior-full-stack-developer

ADDITIONAL CONTEXT TO ADD (Optional field in the form):

Company Culture:
PeopleLogic has a startup culture with a focus on innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We value work-life balance and believe in empowering our team members to make decisions. Our engineering team follows agile practices with 2-week sprints.

Team Structure:
You'll be joining a team of 15 engineers split into 3 squads (Frontend, Backend, AI/ML). The engineering team reports to our VP of Engineering and works closely with Product and Design teams.

Growth Opportunities:
Clear path to Lead/Principal Engineer or Engineering Manager roles. We invest heavily in learning and development with conference budgets, online courses, and regular tech talks.

Key Skills to Prioritize:
- Python and Flask experience (we use Flask extensively)
- React expertise (our main frontend framework)
- Experience with AI/ML integration (nice to have)
- Strong system design skills
- Startup experience or fast-paced environment
